WORKSHOPS

UI UX Workshop

The UI/UX workshop was the first event conducted by the IEEE-VESIT for the academic year 2021-2022 which was open to all followed by the UI/UX Competition for members only wherein the winners will be awarded Amazon Vouchers worth Rs 300, 200 and 150 respectively for 1st, 2nd and 3rd position. The Workshop was conducted on the 16th of January, 2021, on the platform, Google Meet.

Cyber Security Workshop

The Cyber Security workshop was conducted on 13th February, 2022. A hands on session was conducted for the participants by Aditya Mohan and Ishan Kharat. Topics covered were Common attack techniques, ethical hacking,pen testing. Future opportunities in this domain were briefly explained.

Middleware and routing Workshop

This workshop on Middleware and routing was conducted on 6th February by Summit Jadhav and Juhi Chhatlani. A news app was made which fetched news using APIs.

Machine Learning Workshop

Being one of the most trending topics in science at the moment, Machine Learning evolves from artificial intelligence and study of pattern recognition. With such a rapid growth and large scope, it would be suffice to conclude that this domain is the future of the world. Hence IEEE-VESIT in collaboration with CSI-VESIT conducts a workshop of Machine learning, providing a platform for the students of our college to learn this subject

Android Workshop

The objective of this workshop was to make people learn to develop android applications using Android Studio with practical implementation, creating a foundation for them to be able to manage one of their own, like a professional.

Technical paper presentation seminar

The Technical Paper Presentation seminar was conducted on 10 th April 2021 . The seminar, which was conducted on Google Meet, taught the participants the importance of technical papers, how to systematically search for them along with techniques to quickly gauge the underlying concept of the research paper. The speaker for the seminar was Mrs. Priya R.L (Assistant Professor, the Department of Computer Engineering).

Blockchain Technology Workshop

The Blockchain workshop is conducted to give the participants an overview of this in-demand technology, with the help of a hands-on project. With the platform being Google Meet, the participants were taught how to develop an application with the help of blockchain, solidity and Ethereum

Game Development Workshop

A workshop on creating virtual reality games based on the Unity Gaming Engine. The topics that are covered are: getting started with unity, the commands and different constituents of it, how the software collectively works, how objects interact with each other in unity and the setting to build a VR app. Students also get to create a game of their own as a part of a project.

EVENTS

Online Treasue Hunt

Online treasure event is a story-lined based virtual event. It was held on 20th & 21st February , 2021 and was live for 24hrs. It embarked on a plethora of technology’s most cryptic & seemingly unsolvable puzzles, an enduring scavenger hunt that led the players to scratch their heads and put their brilliance & problem-solving capabilities to a very challenging test right down to the wire. The first one to finish was declared the ultimate champion

Case Closed

This event was the one that etched a permanent mark for all participants and spectators. The amphitheater was transfigured into a maze, which depicted the arena, the simulated cyber world called ‘The Matrix’, from the famous blockbuster movie.

Battle of Kingdoms

The Overlord: Battle of Kingdoms was a three-hour event scheduled on 24th April 2021 from 3:30 pm to 6:30 pm. This fun event was designed as well as conducted by the Coordinators of IEEE-VESIT, exclusively for the second-year students. This event was divided into two stages, Stage-1 and Stage-2. The storyline of the event, inspired by various fictional affairs of warriors and kingdoms.

Mastermind

Mastermind is an exciting and fast paced event. It was held 27th September. It evaluated one’s general and technical knowledge in the form of puzzles & questions.

Galactic Showdown

This mega event by IEEE-VESIT was held on the 7th and 8th of February. The first day had teams showcasing their logical and creative skills through a plethora of mini games while the second day involved testing their analytical and strategic skills through buying formidable spaceship, forged alliances, invading planets and establishing colonies.

TROTA

Participants were allowed to take part in teams of 2 to 3 and their strategic prowess was put to test. The first day had teams showcasing their logical and creative skills through a plethora of mini games designed while the second day involved testing their analytical and strategic skills through buying formidable spaceship, forged alliances, invade planets and establish colonies.

Road Show

In today’s world, the need of inventors has risen exponentially. Hence, this event is organized every year by IEEE-VESIT in order to encourage young minds to be creative pioneers in order to tackle modern problems. The participants are given 7 domains to choose from, namely - agriculture, defense, energy conservation, healthcare, transportation, home and industrial automation, and smart city services.

COC

CoC was held on 21st and 22nd Jan. This strategic card game saw students across all years battling it out to emerge as the winners. It involved currency, various action cards and attack cards, all of which were focused towards buying Victory cards, namely Estates, Dutchy and Province.

Quizzard

This exciting and fast paced event was held on 16th January. This quiz competition had three levels with increasing difficulties and alluring activities so that the participants were gripped throughout. This was an event to evaluate one’s general and technical knowledge in the form of a quiz

MUN

It was conducted on 12th and 13th February, 2020. Participants in teams of two were assigned delegations of different countries in this years’ simulation of the United Nations Security Council. The theme was crisis between the USA and Iran and also extended to the Gulf of Hormuz’s situation.
